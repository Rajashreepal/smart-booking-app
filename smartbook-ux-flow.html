⏱ Overlap Algorithm (timeToMinutes)
Convert HH:mm → minutes. Conflict if: slotStart < existingEnd AND slotEnd > existingStart
Example: 09:30 = 570 min, 10:00 = 600 min → overlap check is purely arithmetic, no date library needed.
